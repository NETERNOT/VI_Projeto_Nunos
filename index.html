Info

"Visualizing Metal" is a data visualization project whose objetive is to identify the relationships between Metal bands and Metal genres.

This is an academic project made for the "Information Visualization" course unit of the Master's in Design and Multimedia from the Faculty of Science and Technology from the University of Coimbra.

Authors: Nuno Flórido & Nuno Pinto
